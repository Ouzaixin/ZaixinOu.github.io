Selected Projects

Synthesizing A$\beta$-PET from MRI using Latent Diffusion Models
Performed image preprocessing on over 1,500 paired MRI and A$\beta$-PET scans for AI model training and analysis
Developed an {\bf I}mage-and-{\bf L}abel {\bf C}onditioning {\bf L}atent {\bf D}iffusion {\bf M}odel ({\bf IL-CLDM}) to synthesize A$\beta$-PET scans from the corresponding MRI scans, learning both image synthesis and disease diagnosis
The proposed {\bf IL-CLDM} generates realistic A$\beta$-PET scans with superior image quality compared to five SOTA methods, benefiting both A$\beta$ status classification and AD classification
